Our Mission

Mapúa Gaming Society will be an organization committed to providing students exposure to different industries related to gaming, competitive esports, and leadership, giving them their avenue of growth and realizing their true potential. Mapúa Gaming Society seeks to promote a healthy balance in academics and excel towards the advancements in the gaming industry while empowering the principles of a good role model to society and future generations.

Our Vision

Mapúa Gaming Society aims to be the leading gaming organization in Mapúa University, producing competitive, critical thinkers and proficient and effective communicators in the local and international gaming industry, community, and esports.
